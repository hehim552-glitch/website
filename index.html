Project Workflow &
Technical Details

I provide two complimentary rounds of revisions per asset. This covers lighting adjustments, pose tweaks, and minor color grading. Major structural changes or redesigns requested after the final render has been delivered will be subject to a surcharge.
Standard project completion is typically achieved within 3 to 4 Business Days. For time-sensitive projects, a priority queue option is available, guaranteeing delivery within 48 hours for an additional premium.
My studio utilizes an industry-standard stack: Blender 4.0 for high-fidelity path-traced rendering, Roblox Studio for asset extraction, and Adobe Photoshop for professional color grading and VFX composition.
While I do accept commissions without provided models, please be advised that this reduces overall visual quality. I am forced to rely on public toolbox assets which may appear generic. For a unique, high-tier result, custom models are essential.
Clients receive high-resolution, unwatermarked PNG files. Please note that PSD (Photoshop) source files are not provided under standard licensing to protect studio proprietary techniques and workflow.
